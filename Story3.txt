Amidst the cheerful chaos of Disney World, a disenchanted park employee contemplates the meaning of happiness while cleaning up after ecstatic visitors.

He thinks, "Wow, life sucks!"
And then... the Mouse appears...
He says, "How could you say that when stuff like this exists?" He then pulls out a giant cartoon hammer
and hits a kid in the head.
"Wow," the employee thinks, "that IS great!"
